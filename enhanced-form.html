Pharmacy Glossary

Prescription (Rx)
A written order from a healthcare provider for a specific medication.
Generic Drug
A medication that has the same active ingredients as a brand-name drug but is typically less expensive.
Pharmacokinetics
The study of how a drug is absorbed, distributed, metabolized, and excreted by the body.
